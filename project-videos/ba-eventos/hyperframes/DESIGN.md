# Design

## Style Prompt

Use the BA Eventos product surface as the proof anchor: dark, warm, focused, and search-driven. The video should feel like a short product demo for a visitor, not a technical architecture tour. Show the messy source problem, the natural-language question, the grounded answer, and the useful event result. Keep text simple and readable.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#0b0705`
- Panel: `#17100c`
- Raised panel: `#211610`
- Ink: `#f7ead9`
- Muted text: `#b9aa99`
- Border: `#3b2a1f`
- Amber: `#f3ab35`
- Amber soft: `#3a2511`
- Grounded teal: `#69c9bf`
- Catalog green: `#95c47f`

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`, serif
- Interface/body: `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, sans-serif
- Data labels: `SF Mono`, `Menlo`, monospace

## Motion

- Five short demo scenes plus a settled ending beat.
- Use the product screenshots as proof, not decoration.
- Use restrained push and fade transitions.
- Highlight the query, answer, and result cards only where they clarify the story.
- The final end card should settle, then hold for `2.5-3s` before the fade.

## Privacy And Content Guardrails

- Use redacted HyperFrames assets, not the selected originals.
- Keep stack names, database names, model names, APIs, command output, and test counts off-screen.
- Keep every scene to one idea.
- Keep the screenshot readable when it is the proof surface.
