# Project Video Brief

Project: Casamo

Video type: Product walkthrough video

Target duration: 41-42 seconds

Primary viewer: Nontechnical portfolio viewer or product/customer viewer

Story mode: Product walkthrough

One-sentence job: Casamo helps remote workers choose long-stay accommodations by checking listing evidence and returning a ranked stay report.

Viewer takeaway: This is a founder-owned product that turns a noisy stay search into a bookable shortlist with evidence, tradeoffs, and host questions.

Plain story:

- Problem: remote workers cannot trust marketplace filters alone when booking furnished stays for weeks at a time.
- Solution: Casamo scans candidate listings against photos, amenities, reviews, missing proof, and trip must-haves.
- Result: the traveler gets one recommended stay, two backups, visible evidence, concerns, and the host questions to ask before booking.

Scene arc:

1. Problem: long-stay bookings are high-trust decisions, but marketplace filters do not prove the stay.
2. Decision support: the traveler needs evidence before paying.
3. Intake: Casamo collects destination, dates, budget, and must-haves.
4. Audit: Casamo keeps the recommendation checkable by preserving signals, concerns, gaps, and host questions.
5. Result: the report gives one recommended stay, two backups, and a clear next question.
6. Product state: Casamo is a focused founder-owned product with paid scans, saved reports, and auditable recommendations.

Reading-speed constraint:

- Use one headline plus one short sentence at most.
- Keep most scenes below 16 must-read words.
- Hold each product image long enough to inspect.
- Keep implementation terms out of the on-screen copy.

Assets needed:

- Screenshot: public portfolio Casamo homepage image.
- Screenshot: public Casamo sample report / OG image.
- Screenshot: public sample report apartment image.
- UI clip: not needed; motion is handled in HyperFrames around static public assets.
- Artifact: native audit and shortlist cards built in HyperFrames.
- Diagram/data point: trip scan pass and workflow cards built in HyperFrames.

Privacy notes:

- Hide: private accounts, customer data, payments, analytics, background jobs, tokens, local paths, and non-public listings.
- Safe to show: public portfolio screenshot, public sample-report images, public Casamo name, public Trip Scan Pass framing, and plain workflow labels.
