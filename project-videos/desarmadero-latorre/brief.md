# Project Video Brief

Project: Desarmadero Latorre

Video type: Catalog/workflow walkthrough loop

Target duration: 21 seconds

Primary viewer: Hiring manager or general portfolio viewer

Story mode: Short product walkthrough

One-sentence job: Desarmadero Latorre turns phone-heavy stock checks into a searchable digital catalog with a clearer inquiry path.

Viewer takeaway: This is a practical workflow improvement: customers search first, inspect part context, and contact the shop with a better question.

Plain story:

- Problem: customers had to call or visit to ask whether a part or vehicle was available.
- Solution: a searchable catalog made stock visible by make, model, and year.
- Result: buyers could arrive with a more specific question, and staff spent less time repeating basic availability checks.

Scene arc:

1. Problem: stock checks started by phone.
2. Searchable surface: the catalog made inventory searchable.
3. Part information: listings show photos, year, and vehicle context.
4. Clearer workflow: search, inspect, then contact.
5. Ending beat: Desarmadero Latorre as a searchable digital stockroom.

Reading-speed constraint:

- Keep each scene to one headline and one short support line or label group.
- Use the public catalog screenshot as the proof anchor.
- Hold the final result card for roughly three seconds.

Assets needed:

- Screenshot: public catalog surface from the portfolio case study.
- Photo: public stockroom photo for the old workflow/business context.
- UI clip: not needed; motion is built in HyperFrames around static public assets.
- Diagram/data point: simple search-to-contact workflow rail built natively in HyperFrames.

Privacy notes:

- Hide: private inventory admin views, customer contact details, account data, tokens, cookies, and local paths.
- Safe to show: public portfolio screenshots, public site/catalog labels, public project name, and synthetic workflow labels.
