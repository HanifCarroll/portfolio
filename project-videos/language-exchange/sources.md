## Redaction Notes

The video uses public portfolio screenshots with synthetic HablaBA examples plus native HyperFrames labels. The composition does not introduce private runtime accounts, real contact details, notifications, tokens, local paths, or non-public user data. It avoids zooming into profile photos and usernames; screenshots are used as broad product proof surfaces.
