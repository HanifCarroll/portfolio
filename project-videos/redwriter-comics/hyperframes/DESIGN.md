# Design

## Style Prompt

Use a graphic comic-portfolio review style: red canvas, black ink, warm off-white panels, strong red accents, editorial registration marks, and large artwork surfaces. The video should feel like a professional portfolio page in motion, not a generic SaaS demo. Keep copy minimal and let the existing Redwriter artwork/site screenshots carry the proof.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#E8342A`
- Deep ink: `#090606`
- Panel: `#FFF7E8`
- Muted panel: `#F2DEC1`
- Border: `#18100D`
- Accent red: `#FF3C30`
- Accent warm: `#F6B14A`
- Teal artwork accent: `#29B8A6`

## Typography

- Display: `Impact`, `Arial Black`, sans-serif
- Interface/body: `Avenir Next`, `Helvetica Neue`, sans-serif
- Labels: `SF Mono`, `Menlo`, monospace

## Motion

- Four scenes, `16.5s` total.
- Use brisk push transitions between the three story beats.
- Show a slow vertical scroll on the portfolio feature surface to make the static screenshot feel like a real site review.
- Keep text entrances quick and readable.
- Hold the final beat for `3s` with no new proof introduced.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not show private client correspondence, CMS/admin screens, account information, local paths, tokens, or cookies.
- Do not use stack names, CMS names, commands, or test output on screen.
- Do not replace the artwork with generated comic imagery.
- Do not let decoration compete with the public portfolio screenshots.
- Do not use a generic dark SaaS palette or purple-blue gradients.
