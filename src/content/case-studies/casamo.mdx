<CaseSection title="Summary">
	Casamo is a stay-decision tool for remote workers booking furnished stays for weeks at a time.

	It scans Airbnb and Booking.com listings, checks the evidence behind each stay, and turns a noisy marketplace search into a ranked report with one recommended pick, two backups, and the questions still worth asking the host.
</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="About">
	The project grew out of a narrow apartment-search prototype, but the product direction changed once the real buyer problem became clearer.

	Remote workers do not need another generic travel search interface. They need to know whether a stay can actually support living and working for multiple weeks before they commit money, time, and travel plans.
</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="Business Objective">
	The product needed to become a paid SaaS around a decision workflow, not a one-off AI demo.

	The first commercial wedge was a $29 Trip Scan Pass: enter destination, dates, budget, guests, and must-haves, then get a report that ranks the strongest stays by practical long-stay fit.
</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="Product Direction">
	<DecisionBlock title="Compete on skeptical evidence, not generic search.">
		Marketplace filters can say a listing has Wi-Fi, workspace, kitchen, or laundry without proving those claims. Casamo's useful job is to challenge the listing with photos, amenities, text, reviews, missing evidence, and host questions.
	</DecisionBlock>

	<DecisionBlock title="Make the report decision-first.">
		The report leads with the strongest candidate and two backups. Scores and evidence still matter, but the product answers the actual booking question first: where should I investigate next?
	</DecisionBlock>
</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="The Work">
	<DecisionBlock title="Built the intake and paid scan flow.">
		The app collects destination, dates, guests, budget, listing cap, and must-haves, then gates scans behind account access and the Trip Scan Pass.
	</DecisionBlock>

	<DecisionBlock title="Turned listing checks into a persisted pipeline.">
		The scan pipeline discovers Airbnb and Booking.com candidates, audits selected listing photos and evidence, deepens the strongest candidates, ranks them, and saves the final report.
	</DecisionBlock>

	<DecisionBlock title="Made the AI output inspectable.">
		Reports preserve claims, photo evidence, review snippets, missing proof, concerns, and host questions so the user can audit the recommendation instead of blindly trusting a model answer.
	</DecisionBlock>

	<DecisionBlock title="Shipped the real SaaS plumbing.">
		Casamo now has Better Auth accounts, Stripe Checkout and webhooks, scan-credit enforcement, saved reports, PostHog analytics, transactional email, and a VPS deployment with Postgres, Redis, and a background worker.
	</DecisionBlock>
</CaseSection>
