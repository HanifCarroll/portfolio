<CaseSection title="Summary">
	The artist had strong work spread across social media, but editors and publishers needed a cleaner way to review it.

    I built a portfolio hub that made sequential pages, covers, and character work easier to scan and compare.

</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="Business Objective">
	Scattered social links made the artist look less established than the quality of the work.

    The site needed to make commission conversations easier by giving editors one professional home for the portfolio.

</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="The Work">
	<DecisionBlock title="Organized the portfolio around editorial review.">
		The galleries were structured around how editors actually inspect work, so sequential art, covers, and character pieces could each do their job.
	</DecisionBlock>

    <DecisionBlock title="Kept the visual system out of the way.">
    	The site stayed fast and clean so the artwork remained the reason to keep reading.
    </DecisionBlock>

</CaseSection>
