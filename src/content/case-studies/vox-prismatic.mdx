<CaseSection title="Summary">
	Vox Prismatic turned long-form transcripts into a reviewable content operations system.

    The product reduced the bottleneck between raw material and scheduled posts while keeping a human in control before publishing.

</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="About">
	Content teams can record hours of valuable material, then lose momentum because useful ideas stay buried in long transcripts.

    Manual repurposing is slow, maintaining voice across posts is hard, and finding the most shareable moments requires judgment.

</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="Business Objective">
	The product needed to extract useful moments, preserve voice, and make the publishing workflow operational instead of producing generic AI drafts.

    The goal was content intelligence, not automatic posting for its own sake.

</CaseSection>

<CaseSection title="Product Direction">
	<DecisionBlock title="Convert raw expertise into reviewable assets.">
